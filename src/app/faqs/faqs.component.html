Joining

How do I become a member?

You may join as a new member:
  • Online - [insert web link here]
  • By Phone - 314 577 5118 M-F 9am-5pm
  • In Person during regular business hours at the Membership Services Desk at MBG, or at the visitor’s center at either the Sophia M Sachs Butterfly House or Shaw Nature Reserve
  • By Mail - membership application

Can I apply my paid admission to my membership if I decide to join?

Yes, you may apply a portion of your paid admission toward your membership if you decide to join onsite the date of your paid admission ticket. The amount that you can apply depends on the category of membership that you are purchasing. Unfortunately, at this time, we cannot apply this credit online. Please inquire by phone at 314 577 5118, or stop by the Membership Services Desk for more information.

How do I apply the military discount to my membership?

If you have a military ID and would like to join as a member, you can apply our Military Appreciation discount to your membership. The Military Appreciation discount can only be processed onsite, as a valid ID is needed to confirm your status.

Do you offer any other types of discounted Membership?

We offer a senior discount of $5 to the Garden-level membership for members who are ages 65 and older. Also, you may apply the cost of your same-day paid admission toward your membership when joining onsite.

Do the primary and secondary cardholders need to live in the same household?

The 2 cardholders do not need to live in the same household, but the benefits of being a member extend to only those 2-named cardholders. Please keep in mind that all membership correspondence and mailings will be sent only to the primary cardholder.

Do I have to have a secondary cardholder?

With the exception of the Individual-level membership, all membership levels include the option to add a secondary cardholder to the membership. Membership benefits extend only to named cardholders. If your membership includes a secondary cardholder, we strongly recommend that you name a second cardholder to take advantage of all the benefits of the level of membership you selected.

Are children included in membership benefits?

All membership levels include free general admission for all kids age 12 and under. Any person over the age of 12 is considered an adult for admission purposes.

Can my nanny/babysitter bring my children to the Garden using my membership?

No, unless the caretaker is listed as the secondary cardholder on the membership. Membership benefits are not transferrable. Please note that all attendees count towards the total number of admissions allowed per day.

Do you offer a membership for young adults?

The Individual-level membership is a great option for young adults joining as members.

Do you offer a membership for families with adult children?

Membership benefits apply only to the two named cardholders on the membership. Any additional adults admitted on a membership are guests of the named members. Garden membership makes a great gift for adult children to access all the membership has to offer!

I am a person with a disability and need a caregiver to assist me with my visit. What do you recommend?

A personal care attendant counts towards the total number of admissions allowed according to your membership level.

Do you have a lifetime membership level?

We do not offer a lifetime membership and do not plan to offer this in the future.

If you recently joined as a member, here is what to expect:

    I JOINED ONLINE:
  • If you would like to visit soon, please bring a copy of your email confirmation to either the membership desk or the Butterfly House or Shaw Nature Reserve visitor’s center. Permanent membership materials will arrive to you by mail in 2-3 weeks.
    • I JOINED BY MAIL OR PHONE:
  • If you would like to visit soon, please stop by to either the membership desk or the Butterfly House or Shaw Nature Reserve visitor’s center to receive a temporary membership card. Permanent membership materials will arrive to you by mail in 2-3 weeks of the date that we receive your membership donation.

Can I change the name(s) on my membership after joining?

You may change the name(s) on your membership at the time of renewal. If you just joined (within the past four weeks), and wish to change the name(s) on your card, you must return all of your current membership cards to be issued cards with the new name(s).

Can I upgrade to a higher level of Membership?

Yes! With the following options:
    If you have joined within the last 6 months:
  • You may ‘upgrade within cycle’ by contributing the difference between your current membership level and the membership level that you would like to upgrade to. You will keep your previous expiration date. Call us at 314 577 5118 to do so.
    • If you more than 6 months have passed since you have joined as a member:
  • You may renew your membership for an additional year at the upgraded level and you will not lose any time on your membership. Your new expiration date will be 12 months following your current expiration date. You will receive the upgraded benefits for the remainder of your current membership term and through the next term.

Can I downgrade to a different membership level?

The option to downgrade your membership level is available at your time of renewal.

When will my membership expire?

Your Membership is valid for 1 year from the date you join. You can always email membership@mobot.org or call 314 577 5118 to inquire about your expiration date.

Is my membership tax deductible?

  • Yes! 100% of your membership is tax deductible. We will send you a tax receipt with your membership packet within 2-3 weeks of your joining date.
  • If you have joined at the Festival-level or above with the quarterly payment option, we will send you a tax receipt for your previous year’s donations in February.

Renewing

How do I renew?

You may renew your membership:
  • Online - [insert web link here]
  • By Phone - 314 577 5118 M-F 9am-5pm
  • In Person during regular business hours at the Membership Services Desk at MBG, or at the visitor’s center at either the Sophia M Sachs Butterfly House or Shaw Nature Reserve
  • By Mail - membership renewal form

I renewed my membership, now what?

    I RENEWED ONLINE:
  • If your previous membership cards have not yet expired, you may continue to use them until your new cards arrive in the mail. If your cards are not still active and you would like to visit soon, please bring a copy of your email confirmation to either the membership desk or the Butterfly House or Shaw Nature Reserve visitor’s center. Permanent membership materials will arrive to you by mail in 2-3 weeks. Your membership number will remain the same.
    • I RENEWED BY MAIL OR PHONE:
  • If your previous membership cards have not yet expired, you may continue to use them until your new cards arrive in the mail. If your cards are not still active and you would like to visit soon, please stop by to either the membership desk or the Butterfly House or Shaw Nature Reserve visitor’s center to receive a temporary membership card. Permanent membership materials will arrive to you by mail in 2-3 weeks of the date that we receive your membership renewal. Your membership number will remain the same.

Can I change the name(s) on my membership after renewal?

You may change the name(s) on your membership at the time of renewal. If you just renewed (within the past four weeks), and wish to change the name(s) on your card, you must return all of your current membership cards to be issued cards with the new name(s).

When will my membership expire?

If your current membership term is still active, your membership will be renewed for one year after the date of your previous expiration. If your membership has lapsed at the time of renewal, your new membership term is valid for 1 year from the date of renewal. You can always email membership@mobot.org or call 314 577 5118 to inquire about your expiration date.

If I renew my Membership before it expires, how will this affect my expiration date?

  • Your membership is valid for 1 year from the date of renewal. If you renew your membership early, your membership will be renewed for one year after the date of your previous expiration.
  • If you renew your membership at an upgraded level, your new expiration date will be 12 months following your current expiration date. You will receive the upgraded benefits for the remainder of your current membership term and through the next term.
  • If you choose to ‘upgrade within cycle’ and contribute the difference between your current membership level and your upgraded level, your membership is not renewed and your expiration date will remain the same. We will mail you new membership cards at the upgraded level.

Is my membership tax deductible?

  • Yes! 100% of your membership is tax deductible. We will send you a tax receipt with your membership packet within 2-3 weeks of your renewal date.
  • If you have renewed at the Festival-level or above with the quarterly payment option, we will send you a tax receipt for your previous year’s donations in February.

Benefits

What are the benefits of being a member?

Benefits include year-round admission to the Garden, Butterfly House, and Shaw Nature Reserve, 10-20% discounts in our gift shops, including the Little Shop Around the Corner, a subscription to the Bulletin, our quarterly member newsletter, and discounted or free admission to special events. Please click here to view the full list of Member benefits: [LINK HERE]

Do member benefits apply to the guests that I bring in using my membership?

  • Member benefits apply only to named cardholders. Many membership levels include admission benefits for more than two adults. These are guests of the named cardholders.
  • Members-only events include admission for additional guests of members, depending on membership level.

How do I redeem my member thank you gift?

Members who join or renew in person may pick up their thank you gift at the time of renewal (or joining) at any of our locations. Members who join or renew online, by phone, or by mail, will receive a member gift voucher in the mail in their membership packet with their membership cards. You may bring this to the membership desk, the Butterfly House, or the Shaw Nature Reserve to pick up your membership thank you gift.

Do I receive a discount on general admission for my guests?

Your membership level entitles you to a certain amount of guests. Any additional guests will not be admitted at a discount.

Are there other discounts I can receive with my Membership card?

Absolutely! Members receive 10-20% off in our gift shops, including the Little Shop Around the Corner. As well as discounts on Garden classes and admission to special events at the festivals at the Garden.

Usage Guidelines

Membership Terms and Conditions

  • All contributions are final and non-refundable. Memberships may not be sold or otherwise transferred between individuals. Photo ID is required with membership card(s) for entry into the Garden. Discounts on special events and performances apply if membership is active through event date. Member discounts cannot be applied to already discounted items in the Garden Gate Shop.
  • All memberships, regardless of level, are licenses revocable at will by Missouri Botanical Garden. A membership will automatically be revoked if a member, or any guest of a member, breaches the Garden Etiquette and Rules section of MBG’s website [Etiquette and Rules LINK] . the rules of conduct or any other restrictions applicable to a member or any admittee to Missouri Botanical Garden. Memberships are not automatically renewable and shall expire after the membership term, one year, which is specified at the time of renewal/joining. Missouri Botanical Garden may, in its sole discretion and with or without cause, permanently revoke an individual's right to join as a member, and ban or otherwise prevent an individual from attending events or functions at Missouri Botanical Garden or otherwise entering Missouri Botanical Garden. MBG reserves the right to alter, close or remove exhibits without prior notice. Pricing, Terms and Conditions are subject to change without notice.

Membership ID Policy

MBG strives to offer world-class exhibits, educational programs, performing arts and special members-only events just for you, our member. To help us maintain and preserve our membership promise to you, we kindly request that you present proof of identification along with your membership card(s) when visiting the Garden. Please accept our apologies for any inconvenience this may cause you, but our intention is to honor you, our most loyal friends, who generously participate in our membership program.

Who may use my Membership?

Membership cards are non-transferable and can only be used by the named cardholder(s). Guests or family members may not take the place of a named cardholder. Missouri Botanical Garden is a non-profit organization that relies heavily on membership support and attendance revenue to maintain its beautiful grounds and facilities. To preserve the integrity of our membership program and its wonderful benefits for contributing members, we ask our members to help us prevent misuse of the membership card(s). Named cardholders are privy to the benefits of membership.

Who may access my member benefits?

Membership must be active through event date to qualify for member discount. Member guest event tickets beyond the allotted number of guests per membership level must be purchased at full price.

May I visit the Garden without my membership cards?

Yes, you may always access the Garden as a current member without your cards. Visit any Membership or Visitor Services desk in the Garden to verify your current membership status. You will be issued a temporary membership card for that visit so that you may enjoy your member benefits.

What should I do if I’ve lost or misplaced my membership cards?

Contact Member Services or visit any Membership or Visitor Services desk in the Garden to request replacement cards.

How can I make a change to my contact information?

Contact Member Services or visit any Membership or Visitor Services desk in the Garden to update your contact information.

Can I put my Membership on hold?

Memberships to MBG are good for one year from the date of joining or renewing. Because they are a one year membership, we are not able to inactivate for a certain time or a put a hold on a membership. The membership will expire one year from the joining or renewing date.

Should I list my child if he/she is 12 years of age or younger?

Children 12 and under are admitted free of charge and do not need to be listed on membership cards.

Can my nanny/babysitter bring my children to the Garden using my membership?

No, unless the caretaker is listed as the secondary cardholder on the membership. Membership benefits are not transferrable. Please note that all attendees count towards the total number of admissions allowed per day.
